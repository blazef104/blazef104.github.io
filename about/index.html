About

Hi I’m Giulio. I have a BSc in Systems and Network Security from the University of Milan, Italy. In 2018 I participate in a research project on the security of avionics protocols at University of Jyväskylä, Finland.

I have a strong passion for technology, security and programming. I love Python and C++ and I’m really interested in low level code and hardware hacking. In my free time I am also interested in AI and Machine Learning.

Occasionally I play capture the flag competitions trying to understand something.

I have also many other interests like hiking, skiing, climbing, everything about planes and much more.
